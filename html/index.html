About Me

I am a hardware-centric Computer Engineering graduate specializing in embedded systems. I bridge the gap between physical design and logic by crafting custom PCB layouts and writing robust firmware for microcontrollers like STM32. My engineering process is uniquely balanced by a background in heavy-duty framing and fine woodworking; I apply the same structural precision and solution-oriented mindset to my craftsmanship that I do to my circuit designs.
